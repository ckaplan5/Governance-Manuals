5.24 Verify the Comparison Approval and Partial Approval are Configured

Once the Manufacturing Role finishes the Role verify the Review Role’s Data Entry page displays correctly and reject all but the one record that contains values in the value in the Manufacturer Part Number and Manufacture Number fields.

To verify the configurations and do a partial approval:

  1. Click the Data Entry button for the Review Role.
  1. Verify the Comparison Approval displays for the changed record.
  1. Verify the Partial Approval check box displays.
  1. Click the Reject check box 5 records.
  1. Click the Browser Back button.
  2. Click the Approve button, a message displays.
  3. Click the OK button, the Request (Partial Approval) page displays.
  4. Click the Edit button.
  5. Enter Testing Partial Approval in the Rejection Reason field.
  6. Click the Save button.
  7. Click Reject .
