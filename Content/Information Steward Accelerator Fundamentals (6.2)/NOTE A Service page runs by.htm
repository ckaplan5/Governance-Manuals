NOTE:A Service page runs, by default every 15 minutes, and creates the request if all validations pass. To manually run the service page click Admin > Resources > Service Pages. Locate dspCompose Service External (External Role Processor) and click the Start button. The next person in the workflow receives an email notification that their Role is ready to be processed. To modify the Service page configurations click Admin > Resources > Service Pages > dspCompose >Service External (External Role Processor).
