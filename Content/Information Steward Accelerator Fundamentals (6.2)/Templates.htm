Templates

Templates are configured by developers to create a controlled reusable process for a business user to remediate data based on one or more ISA reports.

  • A Template represents a process to drive the remediation to post to SAP and is based on a BDC or GUI recording.
  • Templates are recorded by users who are familiar with SAP system.
  • Are connected to one or more ISA Rules by creating an ISA External Request for each Template.
  • Templates are configured to ensure data gets into SAP business ready based on the business scenario and ISA report.
  • Templates are configured with Roles that define who maintains what, who reviews and who posts the data.
  • Templates use the Integrate component to post data to SAP.
  • A Template, like a business process, has unique configuration, validation, business rules, and workflows. It is highly customizable and can be configured for complicated request scenarios.
  • Multiple Requests can be created based on one Template.
