NOTE:To add a role dependency to a template role, the Template must not be active or must be in Developer Mode.

The Manufacturing Role is dependent on the Data role. The Review and Post roles need to be dependent on the new Manufacturing Role.

To setup a dependency to a role:

  1. Locate ManufacturingXX Role.
  1. Click Dependencies , the Template (Role Dependency Select) page displays in the Dependent pane.
  2. Click Depends check box for the Data Role.
