3.15 Generate Requests from ISA

The Template has been configured for the NAME2 and Name 3 ISA reports. Generate the Requests from ISA and view the Requests in dspCompose.

To generate the Requests:

  1. Click the BackOffice Site link.
  1. Click Information Steward Accelerator on the Navigation pane.
  1. Click Report Data Viewer on the Navigation pane.
  1. Click ISA Training – Customer Master – TrainingXX, the Reports display in the Dependent pane.
  1. Locate Customer NAME2 Missing for TrainingXX Report.
  1. Click Create Request in dspCompose , a message displays.
  1. Click the OK button.
  1. Click Compose Request , the Request page in dspCompose displays.
  1. Click the Browser Back button.
  1. Locate Customer NAME3 Missing for TrainingXX Report.
  1. Click Create Request in dspCompose , a message displays.
  1. Click Compose Request , the Request page in dspCompose displays.
  1. Click Requests on the Navigation pane.
