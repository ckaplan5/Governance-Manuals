Once a Request has been generated from ISA, a notification in DSP and/or a workflow email is sent to the person responsible for updating the missing Postal Code fields.

Workflow is a notification method called from within a WebApp when a role is finished. It serves as a means to deliver tasks by sending emails and/or notifications within DSP to users assigned to a particular role. When one stage of work has been completed for a request, an email is sent notifying all members of the subsequent role(s) that they have a role to complete. Links are provided in the email to trigger specific events and to launch the relevant page in the WebApp.

When a role is finished by the person responsible, an email notification is sent to the next person(s) responsible for subsequent role(s).

To process the request:

  1. Open Mozilla Thunderbird on the desktop.
  2. Open the dspCompose Role ready for processing: Customer_AddressesXX - RoleID: Data email.
  3. Review the Workflow email indicating the Data Role is ready to be processed.
