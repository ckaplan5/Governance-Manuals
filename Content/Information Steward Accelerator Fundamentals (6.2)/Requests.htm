Requests

Requests are used by a business user to manage the data entry, review and posting of data into a target ERP system such as SAP.

  • Knowledge of SAP is not required.
  • Requests are based on one or more ISA Reports to remediate failed data in a controlled process and validated to ensure target and business readiness.
  • Requests are workflow-enabled.
  • A workflow is a notification method sent from a component within the ISA or one of its components.
  • Users are assigned to Roles that drive the workflow based on the dependencies assigned to each role for a single reusable process called a Template.
  • Requests are based on a single Template configured for the business scenario to fix the failed data from ISA.
