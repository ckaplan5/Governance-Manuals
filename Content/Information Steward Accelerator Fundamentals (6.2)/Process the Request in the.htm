1.4 Process the Request in the dspCompose Component of ISA

A role is a single step in the business process. A role type is assigned to a role to define how the role data within the request is manipulated. Users will only the data and roles they have access to. Three Global Roles are added by default when a Template is created: Data, Review and Post.

  • Data - Gathers or makes changes to data.
  • Review - Evaluates and reviews data and is responsible for approving or rejecting work. Data can be viewed but not modified.
  • Post – Posts data to the target system, and the final role in the process after the Data and Review roles have been completed.
