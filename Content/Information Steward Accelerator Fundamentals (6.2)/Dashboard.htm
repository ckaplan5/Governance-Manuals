1.1 Review Report in Information Steward Accelerator

The Reports are organized within Projects. The rules from Information Steward are imported into ISA and are assigned to a Project. Each Project must have at least one Distribution, which are groups of users.

When the Distribution is created, the Distribution Owner is automatically registered as a user for the Distribution. The Distribution Owner is the primary contact of information for the data quality rules configured in the Distribution.

Additional users can be added to the Distribution. Once a rule is run in IS and ISA the users assigned to the Distribution receive an email regarding the failed data.

For the purpose of this exercise, the Project and Distribution has already been set up.

To view the report:

  1. Click Report Data Viewer on the Navigation pane.
  2. Click ISA Training – Customer Master 01 in the Parent pane.
