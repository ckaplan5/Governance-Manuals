Review Role

  1. Locate the 4321 Postal Code value.
  1. Click the Reject check box for the record.
  1. Click the Browser Back button.
  1. Click Approve button, a message displays indicating a new Request will be created for the rejected record.
  1. Click the OK button, the Request (Partial Approval) page displays.
  1. Click the Edit button.
  1. Enter Incorrect Postal Code in the Rejection Reason field.
  1. Click the Save button.
  1. Click Reject .
