NOTE:dspCompose™ parameters are high-level fields used throughout the component that must be configured per installation. The Templates tab on the Parameters page under Configuration on the Navigation pane controls the defaults for various settings when a Template is created.

Enable Comparison Approval and Partial Approval for the Review Role.

To configure the settings for the Review Role:

  1. Click Vertical View for the Review Role on the Template (Role) page.
  1. Click Approve and Finish Settings tab.
  2. Click Use Comparison Approval Page check box to enable.
  3. Click Allow Partial Approval check box to enable.
  4. Close the Vertical View.
