Field Technical Information

NOTE:The Screen Number is 4004 and the field name is MARA-BISMT.

  1. Close SAP GUI.
  1. Return to DSP.
  1. Highlight BDC Screen 4004, Priority 30.
  1. Click the Add button in the Dependent pane.
  1. Enter 85 in the Priority field.
  1. Enter MARA-BISMT in Field Name field.
  1. Enter Old in Field Value field.
  1. Click the Save button.
