4.2 Demonstration – Process Project Distribution Rules

The Process Rules button processes the rules immediately. The Process Rules button runs all the rules per the distribution list. This button generates the error reports based on the rules assigned to the distribution list. Every hour a service page runs and processes all rules.

The instructor will process the rules.

To process rules:

  1. Expand Configuration on the Navigation pane.
  1. Click Parameters on the Navigation pane.
  2. Click Process Rules button, a message displays.
  3. Click Ok button.
