NOTE:The dspCompose Category is automatically added when dspCompose is installed. When a Template in dspCompose is generated, an Integrate Template and Integrate Process are automatically added to the dspCompose Category. dspCompose Templates align with an Integrate Template and a dspCompose Request aligns with an Integrate Process.

  1. Click the Add button to add a new Category.
  2. Enter TrainingXX in the Category field.
  3. Enter Material Master in the Description field.
  4. Click the Save button.
  5. Click Templates .
  6. Enter MaterialMasterXX in the Template Name field.
