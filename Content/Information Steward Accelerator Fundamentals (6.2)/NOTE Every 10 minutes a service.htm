NOTE:Every 10 minutes a service page runs that checks if the rules have been run in IS and sends the workflow email if they have.

Configure how the user receives a workflow on the Users page. The settings determines if and how a user receives workflow emails regarding failed data records returned from IS rules. Options are: Configure at the Distribution Level, Do Not Send Workflows, and Send Summary.

ISA retains a history of workflows sent to users for 7 days, based on the default setting in the Days to Retain History field on the Parameters page’s Basic Settings tab.

The content in the body of the workflow can be modified on the Summary Settings tab on the Parameters page under Configuration on the Navigation pane.
