3.1 Review Rules in IS

Information Steward contains key data domains where there are a number of dimensions, validation rules and data sources that contain the data that will be imported into ISA. Typically, a data steward or business user will be monitoring Information Steward on a daily basis and drilling down into each of these rules, rule by rule, to investigate for failures.

The Rules are broken down into quality dimensions and quality dimensions are broken down into validation rules.

After logging in, users will understand the Scorecard and Projects that make up the scorecard in Information Steward. The rules and binding rules associated the Project will be run and pulled into the ISA.

To review the rules:

  1. Double-click Information Steward on the desktop.
  1. Enter the System address in System field.
  2. Enter TrainingXX in User ID field.
