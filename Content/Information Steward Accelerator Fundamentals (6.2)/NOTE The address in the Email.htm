NOTE:The address in the Email From fields for the EmailValidation event and the ExternalData event are identical by default. These addresses must be configured on site and should contain the same email address.

This setup configures dspCompose™ to send a workflow email and any emails about email validation failures to an external contact from the external data email account address. The external contact then replies to the correct email address when submitting data for a request.

To update the Email From fields (Demonstration):

  1. Click Workflow Message on the Navigation pane.
  1. Locate English.
  1. Click Messages .
  1. Locate ExternalData Event.
  1. Click Edit .
  1. Update the Email From field.
