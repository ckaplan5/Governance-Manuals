1.6 Fix the Failed Records

The failed records can be updated on the Data Entry page or downloaded to an Excel spreadsheet. If downloaded to a spreadsheet and updated, the spreadsheet is then uploaded back into dspCompose and the changes are viewable on the Data Entry page and can go through the workflow processes.

To fix the failed records:

  1. Click the Browser Back button.
  1. Click Vertical View for the Data Role.
  1. Click Download Request Data , an orange Notification displays.
