4.5 Generate a Template

The Template must be generated before the ISA External Scenario and any data controls can be configured.

Clicking Generate on the Templates page’s Vertical View, dspCompose creates the objects used by the Template, including pages, stored procedures, tables, views, Integrate Templates and Integrate processes. When the Template is deleted, dspCompose™ deletes all system-generated objects used by the Template.

Two databases and WebApps are installed when dspCompose™ is installed: the cMass and cMass_Data databases, and the dspCompose and cMass_Data WebApps. WebApp dspCompose and its database cMass contain all the settings and validations that come with the installation and should not be modified. Make All customizations and create data validations in the cMass_Data WebApp and database to prevent customizations from being overwritten during any upgrades.
