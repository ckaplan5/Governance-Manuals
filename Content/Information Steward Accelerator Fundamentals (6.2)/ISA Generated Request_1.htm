NOTE:The Data, Review and Post Roles all display because the user has security for all Roles. In a production environment a user may not have access to all Roles. Based on the configured workflow, the Data role is the only role enabled. The Data role must be finished before the Review role becomes enabled. Once the Review role is complete, the Post role becomes enabled. Each role will receive a notification when it is their turn to work on the Request.
