NOTE:If rules were just added to IS, the IS repository in Collect must be refresh before the rules can be added to a Project Distribution in ISA.

To add rules to a Distribution:

  1. Locate Customer Master – TrainingXX in the Dependent pane.
  2. Click Quality Dimensions in the Dependent pane.
  1. Click ISA Training Completeness in Parent frame.
  2. Highlight Customer NAME3 for TrainingXX in the Dependent pane.
  1. Click Add .
  1. Click the Browser Back button.
  1. Locate Customer Master – TrainingXX in the Dependent pane.
  1. Click Rules .
