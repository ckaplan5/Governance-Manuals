Recording Tips

Do not keep recordings with errors. A recording should be as perfect as possible. It should not have any error messages and there should only be warning messages when necessary. If a mistake is made while entering data, stop recording and try again.

There are three ways to capture a field in a dspCompose™ recording:

  • Put a space at the end of the field and delete it.
  • Enter the same value in the field.
  • Change the value in the field.
