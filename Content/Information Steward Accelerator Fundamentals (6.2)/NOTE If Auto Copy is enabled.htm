Add the ManufacturingXX Role to the MaterialMasterUpdateXX Template.

To add a Role to a Template:

  1. Click Home on the Navigation pane.
  2. Click Team on the Navigation pane.
  3. Click Templates for Material Master.
  4. Locate MaterialMasterUpdateXX Template.
  5. Click Roles for the Template.
  6. Click the Add button.
  7. Enter 15 in the Priority field.
