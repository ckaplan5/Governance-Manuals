Templates are organized in Teams. A Team is a grouping of Templates and is used for organizational and management purposes. Any user can see a Team but will not have access to any Templates within the Team unless security for the Template is granted within dspCompose™.

Add the missing NAME2 and NAME3 rules to the Customer Addresses Template used to fix the missing postal codes in Exercise 1.

To add the rules:

  1. Click Team on the Navigation pane.
  2. Locate Customer Master Team.
  3. Click Templates for Customer Master.
  4. Locate Customer_AddressesXX Template.
  5. Click Activate to deactivate the Template.
